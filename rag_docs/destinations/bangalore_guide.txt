Bangalore Travel Guide

Overview:
Bangalore (Bengaluru) is known as the Silicon Valley of India, blending modern tech hubs with gardens, heritage sites, and vibrant food culture.

Top Attractions:
- Lalbagh Botanical Garden
- Cubbon Park
- Bangalore Palace
- Vidhana Soudha
- UB City
- Nandi Hills (day trip)

Recommended 3-Day Plan:
Day 1: Cubbon Park, Vidhana Soudha, MG Road
Day 2: Lalbagh, Bangalore Palace, Church Street
Day 3: Nandi Hills sunrise, local shopping, cafes

Approximate Budget:
Budget trip: 4000-7000 INR
Comfort trip: 8000-15000 INR
Premium trip: 18000+ INR

Best Time:
October to February
