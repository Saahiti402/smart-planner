Mysore Travel Guide

Overview:
Mysore is known for royal heritage, palaces, silk, sandalwood, and cultural landmarks.

Top Attractions:
- Mysore Palace
- Chamundi Hills
- Brindavan Gardens
- St. Philomena's Church
- Mysore Zoo

Recommended 2-Day Plan:
Day 1: Palace, Zoo, local market
Day 2: Chamundi Hills, Brindavan Gardens

Approximate Budget:
Budget trip: 3000-6000 INR
Comfort trip: 7000-12000 INR
Premium trip: 15000+ INR

Best Time:
October to March
