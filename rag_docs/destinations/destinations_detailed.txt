=== CITY: GOA ===
Overview:
Goa is one of India's most popular coastal destinations, known for its beaches, nightlife, Portuguese heritage, seafood, and water sports.

Best Time to Visit:
October to February

Popular Attractions:
- Baga Beach
- Calangute Beach
- Anjuna Beach
- Fort Aguada
- Basilica of Bom Jesus
- Dudhsagar Falls
- Dona Paula
- Panjim Latin Quarter

Suggested Itinerary:
Day 1: North Goa beaches, water sports, nightlife
Day 2: Fort Aguada, churches, Panjim city walk
Day 3: South Goa beaches, cruise, shopping

Food Recommendations:
- Fish curry rice
- Prawn balchao
- Bebinca
- Goan vindaloo

=== CITY: MYSORE ===
Overview:
Mysore is famous for royal heritage, palaces, silk, sandalwood, gardens, and rich cultural traditions.

Best Time to Visit:
October to March

Popular Attractions:
- Mysore Palace
- Brindavan Gardens
- Chamundi Hills
- Mysore Zoo
- St. Philomena's Church
- Karanji Lake

Suggested Itinerary:
Day 1: Mysore Palace and Zoo
Day 2: Chamundi Hills and local markets
Day 3: Brindavan Gardens and museums

Food Recommendations:
- Mysore masala dosa
- Mysore pak
- Filter coffee
- Bisi bele bath

=== CITY: CHENNAI ===
Overview:
Chennai is known for Marina Beach, temples, South Indian culture, classical music, and business travel.

Best Time to Visit:
November to February

Popular Attractions:
- Marina Beach
- Kapaleeshwarar Temple
- Fort St. George
- San Thome Basilica
- Mahabalipuram day trip
- Government Museum

Suggested Itinerary:
Day 1: Beach and temple visit
Day 2: Museum, shopping, food trail
Day 3: Mahabalipuram day trip

Food Recommendations:
- Idli and sambar
- Chettinad chicken
- Filter coffee
- Pongal
