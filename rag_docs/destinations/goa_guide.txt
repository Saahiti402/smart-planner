Goa Travel Guide

Overview:
Goa is famous for beaches, nightlife, Portuguese heritage, seafood, and water sports.

Top Attractions:
- Baga Beach
- Calangute Beach
- Fort Aguada
- Basilica of Bom Jesus
- Dudhsagar Falls
- Panaji Latin Quarter

Recommended 3-Day Plan:
Day 1: North Goa beaches
Day 2: Fort Aguada and Panaji
Day 3: South Goa beaches and cruise

Approximate Budget:
Budget trip: 5000-9000 INR
Comfort trip: 10000-18000 INR
Premium trip: 20000+ INR

Best Time:
November to February
